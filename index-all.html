About The Iron Yard

The Iron Yard Academy teaches intensive, three-month courses that mentor students into professional junior-level programmers. Our program goes far beyond tactical skills and teaches students to think like a software engineer. Students have spent the past twelve weeks studying either Ruby on Rails Engineering or Front End Engineering and today they will present their final projects, which showcase the skills they have learned.

Interested in hiring a graduate or taking a class?

Interested in hiring a graduate?

All of our graduates are ready for their first jobs as junior developers. If you meet someone who would be a great fit for your team, please feel free to reach out to them directly. Graduates' contact info can be found here. If you have questions or want more information about how our job placement program works, get in touch with the local Campus Director or email us at hello@theironyard.com.

Refreshments will be available during the demo. We hope to see you there!

Interested in taking a class or learning more?

Our next classes begin August 24, 2015. We are taking applications now and have room left in both Ruby on Rails Engineering and Front End Engineering. Applications can be found on our website: theironyard.com/apply. We also offer free classes for kids and host a variety of meet-ups and free events.
